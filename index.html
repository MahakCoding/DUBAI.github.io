Dubai is the most populated city in the United Arab Emirates (UAE) and of the 7 monarchies which together form the United Arab Emirates.Established in the 18th century as a small fishing village, the city grew rapidly in the early 21st century with a focus on tourism and luxury,having the second most five-star hotels in the world, and the tallest building in the world, the Burj Khalifa, which is over a half a mile tall.
