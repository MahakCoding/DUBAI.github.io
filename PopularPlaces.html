Destination Details
Burj Khalifa is the tallest building in the world and is over 828 metres (2,716.5 feet) and more than 160 stories.
Burj Al Arab is the tallest all-suit hotel; it also has the world most expensive coctail.
The Atlantis is the world's biggest water park willed with over 70 fun and exciting rides to experiance.
The Dubai Mall is the biggest mall in the world, packed with countless shops and brands.
The Dubai Parks and Resorts is a great attraction especially for kids from the age of 7 to adults. The Dubai Parks include 4 different parks: Motiongate, Bollywood park, Legoland and Legoland Waterpark. They also have resorts to stay at.
